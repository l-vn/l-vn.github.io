About

After a MSc in Mathematics obtained from the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL), I decided to go on with academic research and spent three months as a research intern at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich (ETHZ) in the DISCO group. Finally, I started a PhD in Operations Research in France in Lille (Inocs team at Inria) and Perpignan (PROMES lab) under the supervision of Luce Brotcorne and Didier Aussel.

My research subject consists in establishing optimal electricity pricing strategies for demand side management. To this purpose, bilevel programs are being used.
