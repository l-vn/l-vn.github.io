About

After a MSc in Mathematics obtained from the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL), I decided to go on with academic research and spent three months as a research intern at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich (ETHZ) in the DISCO group. Finally, I started a PhD in Operations Research in France in Lille (Inocs team at Inria) and Perpignan (PROMES lab) under the supervision of Luce Brotcorne and Didier Aussel.

The title of my PhD is "Design and Pricing of New Energy Services in a Competitive Environment". More precisely, I try to establish optimal electricity pricing strategies for demand side management by creating multi-level optimization models. Until now, I considered single-leader single-follower and single-leader multi-follower models. I intend to switch to multi-leader multi-follower models in a soon future.

Apart from research, I am a passionate tennis player, an enthusiastic reader, a lover of puns and a defender of Oxford commas.
